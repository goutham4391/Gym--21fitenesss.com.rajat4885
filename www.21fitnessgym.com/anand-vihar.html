About Gym

21 Fitness is a GYM ENTERPRISE that promotes workouts with a touch of luxury, provides a safe, functional, and comfortable workout environment, and creates a pleasant atmosphere for recreation and socialization.

We deeply emphasize quality equipment, the safety and comfort of our members, the availability of modern amenities, aesthetic appeal of the facility, the proper maintenance of the premises, the competence and friendly attitude of the staff members, and proper utilization of the space.

There are many fitness machines, exercise accessories, and enough of the "popular" machines (like treadmills), and hence, there is no time limit to use any specific equipment. 21 Fitness has both cutting-edge and traditional equipment.
